The relationship between information systems and organizational culture is a significant area of focus in Information Systems Research. Researchers analyze how organizational culture influences the acceptance and usage of information systems among employees. Understanding these dynamics is essential for successful technology adoption and change management. Studies reveal that a supportive culture can enhance user engagement with systems, leading to increased productivity and satisfaction. Furthermore, ISR explores the role of leadership in shaping an organization's culture to foster an environment conducive to technological innovation. This intersection of culture and technology is crucial for realizing the full potential of information systems in modern enterprises.
