Data analytics and big data have become central themes in Information Systems Research, driving insights into customer behavior and operational efficiency. ISR examines the methodologies used to collect, process, and analyze large datasets, emphasizing the importance of data-driven decision-making. Researchers explore various analytical techniques, including predictive analytics, machine learning, and data mining, to uncover patterns and trends that inform strategic initiatives. The ethical implications of data usage and privacy concerns are also critical areas of investigation, as organizations strive to balance innovation with responsible data management practices. This evolving field continues to challenge researchers to develop new frameworks that address these complexities in a rapidly changing digital landscape.
